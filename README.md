## FAQ

### How do I download a Pornhub video with this extension?

Open the video, click the downloader icon, choose your preferred resolution, and
let the progress bar finish. The MP4 will save using the original title in your
downloads folder.

### Does it work with Pornhub Premium?

Yes. Stay logged into your Premium account and the extension will access
subscriber-exclusive streams using your active session—no extra setup required.

### Can I download from Thumbzilla too?

Absolutely. Thumbzilla uses similar players, and the extension detects and
downloads those streams using the same workflow.

### Will it capture captions or interactive overlays?

The downloader focuses on video and audio. Captions, overlays, or interactive
elements aren’t included in the MP4 file.

### Which browsers are supported?

Builds are available for Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Brave, and Opera on Windows,
macOS, and Linux desktops. Safari is not supported.

### Does the tab need to stay open during the download?

Yes. Keeping the original tab open ensures Premium sessions stay active and
progress tracking continues uninterrupted.

### Is my activity private?

All conversion happens locally and no analytics are collected. Only the final
MP4 touches your filesystem so your viewing remains private.
